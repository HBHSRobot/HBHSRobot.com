Who We Are

Started and run by Huntington Beach High School Students, the HBHS Robotics Team aims to give students real world experience in robotics, programming, and development.

First Robotics

The HBHS Robotics Team competed in First Robotics Competition 2016, First Stronghold, and is planning on competing in First Steamworks, 2017.

What We Do

The HBHS Robotics Team aims to give interested students real world experiance in STEM fields.

Programming

Team members learn Java and LabVIEW G through real world robotics applications.

Mechanical Engineering

Team members learn mechanical engineering through design, simulation, contruction, and use of robotics.

Electrical Engineering

Team members gain experience with electronics, instrumentation, power, and control through robotics applications.

Team Workflow

Team members gain experience working with organized team workflow and communication.
